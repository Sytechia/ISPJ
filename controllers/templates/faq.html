How long will it take for my order to arrive after I make payment?

Members who ship their orders within Singapore should expect to receive their orders within five (5) to ten (10) working days upon payment verification depending on the volume of orders received.

If you experience delays in receiving your order, contact us immediately at (+65) 68991082 and we will help to confirm the status of your order.

What if I'm not home?

If no one is at the address when delivery is attempted, we will leave the package in a secure location. We'll make three delivery attempts on consecutive days. If the third delivery attempt is unsuccessful your package will be returned to IDEA for a refund.

How much is the shipping fee?

How do you ship my orders?

All your orders are sent via Singapore Post Limited (SingPost).

Where do you ship?

We only ship within Singapore

How long does it take to ship my order?

Once we receive an order, we expect it to ship out one business day later barring any unforeseen issues. If your order is placed over the weekend, this means if standard delivery is selected, we expect that package to ship out no later than Tuesday.

How can I track my orders & payment?

After logging into your account, the status of your orders and order history can be found under the ‘My Orders’ tab in the Account page.

Can I cancel / change my order?

Buyers can only request for cancellation once for every order. As such, once your request is rejected or withdrawn, you would not be able to submit another cancellation request for the same order. Buyers are not allowed to change their orders after payment.

Buyers may cancel their order under the ‘My Orders’ tab in the Account page. Once you have requested for Order cancellation, the order will be automatically cancelled by the system and you will be refunded within 7 to 14 working days.

What if my product is backordered?

If you have questions about your order status, please call IDEA Customer Care at +65 3159 3099.

How do I make payments using PayPal? How does it work?

PayPal is the easiest way to make payments online. While checking out your order, you can choose to pay via PayPal, you will then be redirected to the PayPal website.

Be sure to fill in correct details for fast & hassle-free payment processing. After a successful PayPal payment, your order information will be automatically generated to Idea.com system for your order.

What are the payment methods available?

We accept payment via Visa, Mastercard or Paypal.

Which currency will I be charged in?

All prices listed on our website and billing charges are processed in Singapore Dollars (SGD).

How do I reactivate my account?

If you have disabled your account previously and want to reactivate your account, simply go to the login page and input your login credentials. You will be redirected to a page that allows you to restore your account. Just type in your email and password to reactivate your account.

How can I change my shipping address?

You can change your shipping address under the My Addresses tab in the Account page. You may also choose to add multiple shipping addresses.

By default, the last address added will be used during the checkout process. However, you may choose which address you would want to set as your default by clicking on the ‘Set as default address’ button.

How can I change my credit card details?

You can update your credit card details under the My Wallet tab in the Account page. You may also choose to add credit cards.

By default, the last credit card added will be used during the checkout process. However, you may choose which credit card you would want to set as your default by clicking on the ‘Set as Default Card’ button.

How can I recover a forgotten password?

If you have forgotten your password, you can recover it by clicking on ‘Forgot password’ at the login page. You will receive an e-mail with a link to enter and confirm your new password.

Your password must be alphanumeric with a minimum of 8 characters including at least 2 uppercase letters, 2 numbers and 2 special characters.

What should I do if I am not able to change the password?

Make sure that the password contains at least 8 characters including at least 2 uppercase letters, 2 numbers and 2 special characters. You are also not allowed to reuse any of your previous 5 passwords that you have used before.

What should I do if I receive a faulty / damaged item?

In the event of damaged products received, we will require photo proof of the affected products and your D.O (Delivery Order) in order for us to investigate and review before a decision is made to re-send the product to you at no cost, subject to availability.

Affected products should not be thrown away before taking photo proof and emailing the photo of the affected product and your D.O (Delivery Order) to us at help@idea.com (if applicable). We take such matters very seriously and will investigate individual cases thoroughly.

What should I do if my delivery is lost?

In the event of lost or delayed mail, we will try to locate the delivery team in Singpost and if there is a clear indication that your order is indeed lost, we'll re-send the order to you at no cost, subject to availability.

Please email us your D.O (Delivery Order) at help@idea.com. We take such matters very seriously and will look into individual cases thoroughly.

What if the products I have received is unsatisfactory?

In the event that the products you've received is unsatisfactory in any way you perceive, we will require photo proof of the product and your D.O (Delivery Order) as well and you may be required to send us back the product for close inspection and review before a decision is made to re-send the product to you at no cost, subject to availability. The postage cost will be credited back to your account after we receive the returned item.

Affected products should not be thrown away before taking photo proof and emailing the photo of the affected product and your D.O (Delivery Order) to us at help@idea.com (if applicable). We take such matters very seriously and will investigate individual cases thoroughly.

What if I received the wrong products?

In the event that you've received the wrong products, we will require photo proof of the wrongly sent products and D.O (Delivery Order) and after reviewing, we'll re-send the correct products to you at no cost, subject to availability.

Affected products should not be thrown away before taking photo proof and emailing the photo of the affected product and your D.O (Delivery Order) to us at help@idea.com (if applicable). We take such matters very seriously and will investigate individual cases thoroughly.

What if I received my order with missing products?

In the event you have received your order with missing product(s), we will require you to email us a clear photo proof of your D.O (Delivery Order) to help@idea.com and after which, kindly give us a call at +65 3159 3099 and our customer service officer will attend to you to find out more before a decision is made to re-send the missing product to you at no cost, subject to availability.

We take such matters very seriously and will investigate individual cases thoroughly.

What is your return policy?

IDEA products may be returned within 90 days of the purchase date, provided you retain all packing materials, the product is in 100% resalable condition, and you have your original proof of purchase.

How much does returns cost?

Return costs is completely free.

What is IDEA’s refund policy?

Refund is automatically processed once cancellation has been successfully processed.

How long is the refund process?

Our refund will generally take 5-10 business days to reflect on your bank statement.

How can I check the refund status?

Go to Account > My Orders.
